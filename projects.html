Internship Projects

As part of my internship, I have been part of three group projects with other students in the internship. The various goals and ideas of the projects are all unique in that they each pertain to a different field of computer science work.

Development

The first project in which I participated was a development project. The goal of the project was to redesign a website for the fictional company "MAXX Power" to have a higher visual appeal and smoother functionality. My assigned role was that of Quality Assurance Specialist, meaning that my work involved testing the website's features and seeing what needed to be fixed, as well as writing policies for future Quality Assurance Specialists.
